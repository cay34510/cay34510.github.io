Why you should own a beagle 

Choosing a dog can often be a very hard thing to agree upon and you cant make the choice alone. Here are several reasons why beagles should be your dog of choice and how you can go about getting one! 


Before you adopt a beagle for yourself, its important to know their history and origins. Beagle ancestors were primarily used for hunting in ancient Greece in the 5th century BCE. While they have amazing senses of smell, they weren't the fastest dogs so people started to breed talbot hounds instead and the beagle lost some relevance for some time. Fast forward to the 1870's and a man by the name of General Richard Rowett from Carlinville, Illinois was one of the first people to import Beagles from England to the US to begin establishing a quality bloodline. The breeding continued and the beagle rapidly became a popular breed in the United States. Today beagles still are used for hunting copmanions but have also moved to being showcased in dog shows and is currently named the number three most popular dog breed.
